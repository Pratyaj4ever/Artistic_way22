About Us

Our Mission

At our core, we are driven by a passionate mission to celebrate and preserve the rich heritage of Maithila art and culture. With deep roots in the Mithila region of Bihar, India, and neighboring areas in Nepal, Maithila art has captivated hearts and minds for centuries with its vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and profound storytelling.

Our foremost mission is to preserve and promote the timeless tradition of Maithila painting. By showcasing the exquisite craftsmanship and cultural significance of this ancient folk art form, we aim to raise awareness and appreciation for Maithila art among a global audience. Through curated galleries, educational resources, and community engagement, we strive to ensure that the legacy of Maithila painting endures for generations to come.

Central to our mission is the empowerment of Maithil artists and artisans, particularly women, who have been the custodians of this cherished art form for centuries. We are committed to providing a platform for Maithil artists to showcase their talent, amplify their voices, and gain recognition for their contributions to the art world. By fostering an inclusive and supportive community, we aim to celebrate the diversity and creativity of Maithila art while empowering local communities to thrive.

As advocates for cultural preservation and social impact, we are committed to using Maithila art as a catalyst for positive change. Through collaborations with local communities, cultural organizations, and grassroots initiatives, we seek to leverage the power of art to address social issues, promote sustainable development, and foster cross-cultural understanding. By harnessing the transformative power of Maithila art, we aim to create a more equitable and inclusive world where the beauty of diversity is celebrated and honored.

We invite you to join us on this meaningful journey as we celebrate the beauty, resilience, and cultural richness of Maithila art and culture. Together, let us uphold the legacy of Maithila painting, empower artists and artisans, educate and inspire future generations, and make a lasting impact on the world through the transformative power of art.
